About Us

Twenty years ago, we took our first steps into the industry. Moving between comics, animation, and games, we experienced every stage of the graphics pipeline. That journey made us who we are today. Now, we run a small studio working with a new tool called AI. We live in an era where a few lines of prompt can generate an image. AI has lowered the barrier to design, but paradoxically, the line between true professionals and amateurs has never been clearer. Anyone can create an image now. But there's a real difference between those who settle for AI's first output and those who treat it as a starting point. Maintaining stylistic consistency while capturing emotion. Unifying worldview and brand identity. Selecting the best from countless generated results and refining them to a polished finish. This is where a creator's true skill shows. AI opens possibilities. Twenty years of experience sets the direction. Two people's sensibilities complete the work. From concept to final art, we ensure consistent quality across your entire project—delivered faster and at a more reasonable cost. Game studios, video production companies, advertising agencies—we work with creators building their own worlds.
