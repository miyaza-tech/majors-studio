Introduction

The game art industry stands at a fascinating crossroads. Traditional artistic skills that have been honed over decades are now being augmented by powerful AI tools, creating unprecedented opportunities for creativity and efficiency. At MAJORS STUDIO, we've been at the forefront of this transformation, leveraging our 20 years of industry experience to guide the integration of AI into our production pipeline.
